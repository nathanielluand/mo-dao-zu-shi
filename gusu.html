Gusu Lan Clan

Clan uniform includes white robes with protective incantations stitched into the outer jacket. Direct Lan bloodline members wear white forehead ribbons with the Gusu Lan Clan's cloud motif. Guest disciples, associates, and non-blood related disciples wear plain white forehead ribbons symbolizing self-regulation, with limited touch permission.

History

Gusu Lan Clan was founded by Lan An, who was originally a monk and chose to cultivate as a musician, adopting the surname Lan after "monastery." Lan An founded the clan after finding his "fated person" and becoming their cultivation partner. The Cloud Recesses, the clan's residence, was burned down by the Qishan Wen Clan but later rebuilt with the help of Jin Guangyao. The clan had over 4,000 rules carved into the Wall of Discipline. They owned external establishments like the Bamboo Cottage and had a strained relationship with the Moling Su Clan, led by a former Gusu Lan disciple.
