Qishan Wen Clan

The robes worn by cultivators of the Qishan Wen Clan are white with red hems and sun motifs. High-ranking cultivators wear these "sun robes" with flames around the sleeves and collar.

History

The Qishan Wen Clan was founded by Wen Mao, who introduced the bloodline-based system of cultivation. The third leader of the Qishan Wen Clan erected the Wall of Incantations around the Burial Mounds due to the failure in purging its resentful energy. The Qishan Wen Clan was once the most powerful clan with a large number of disciples, land, and magical tools. However, under Wen Ruohan's rule, they became tyrannical, leading to their downfall in the Sunshot Campaign and the subsequent abuse and killing of surviving members.
