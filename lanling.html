lanling Jin Clan

The Lanling Jin Clan members wear embroidered clothes with a white peony crest, called the Sparks-Amidst-Snow. They also sport a vermilion mark between their eyes, symbolizing their openness to wisdom and their desire to "illuminate the world with vermilion light."

History

Lanling Jin Clan, led by Jin Guangshan, becomes the cultivation world leader after the demise of Qishan Wen Clan. They recruit Demonic Cultivators to gain power and construct twelve-hundred watchtowers to protect neglected commoners. Jin Guangshan's son, Jin Guangyao, becomes the first Chief Cultivator, aiming to correct the clan's past misdeeds. Despite opposition, the watchtowers serve as alerts for suspicious activities and provide support to impoverished commoners. After Jin Guangyao's downfall, Jiang Cheng helps Jin Ling become the new clan leader.
