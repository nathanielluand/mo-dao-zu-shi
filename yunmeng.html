Yunmeng Jian Clan

Those from the Yunmeng Jiang Clan wear purple uniforms. Direct disciples wear a silver bell on their belts, engraved with their signature motif, the nine-petaled lotus.

History

Founded by rogue cultivator Jiang Chi, the Yunmeng Jiang Clan grew to become one of the five great clans. Its disciples are known for their honesty and unrestrained ways. During the Sunshot Campaign, the Yunmeng Jiang Clan is nearly wiped out and their stronghold, Lotus Pier, is seized by the Qishan Wen Clan. Clan Leader Jiang Fengmian and his wife Yu Ziyuan die defending their home. This event plays a significant role in the Sunshot Campaign's overthrow of the Qishan Wen Clan. Under the leadership of Jiang Cheng, the new clan leader, the Yunmeng Jiang Clan launches a siege against Wei Wuxian, resulting in the death of Jiang Cheng's former senior martial brother.
