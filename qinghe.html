Qinghe Nie Clan

is one of the four great cultivation clans and resides in the Impure Realm, Qinghe.

History

The founder of the Qinghe Nie Clan was a butcher. The sixth leader of the Qinghe Nie Clan came up with the idea to turn their Ancestral Hall into Stone Castles to honor the violent saber spirits of their ancestors with a number of corpses that were about to turn into fierce corpses in order to prevent bloodshed.
